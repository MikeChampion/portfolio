Mike Champion

Web Developer, Designer, Board Gamer

Hi, I'm Mike Champion, I'm here to "champion" any web project you have to the finish line! (And, yes, I'm fluent in puns, word play, and dad jokes.)

While web development is a fairly new career aspiration for me, I have years of learning via Google for positions where I was the entire IT department and experience managing people and cultivating relationships.

In my free time, I play tabletop board games, which involves digging in and learning new rules, and explaining them to the group (and then re-explaining some rules the right way once we discover we've been doing something wrong).
